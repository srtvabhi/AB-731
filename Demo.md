
# Microsoft Copilot Chat 

## Audience
Business leaders, executives, and senior managers

## Objective
Demonstrate how Microsoft Copilot Chat helps leaders scale expertise, improve decision-making,
and drive business outcomes.

---

## Demo 1: Executive Briefing from Scattered Information

### Scenario
Prepare for an executive meeting using information spread across emails, Teams chats, and documents.

### Copilot Prompt
Create a 1-page executive briefing on the Q3 customer churn issue.
Use emails, Teams messages, and shared documents from the last 30 days.
Highlight key drivers, risks, and recommended next steps.

### Business Value
- Faster executive readiness
- Better-informed decisions
- Less time searching for information

---

## Demo 2: Scaling Subject Matter Expertise

### Scenario
Translate complex financial insights for non-finance leaders.

### Copilot Prompt
Based on our financial reports and dashboards, explain the top 3 reasons
revenue growth slowed last quarter.
Use simple language suitable for non-finance leaders.

### Business Value
- Consistent insights
- Reduced dependency on experts
- Faster alignment

---

## Demo 3: Strategic Scenario Analysis

### Scenario
Evaluate expansion into a new market.

### Copilot Prompt
Summarize the pros, cons, and risks of expanding into the APAC market
based on our internal strategy documents and past expansion efforts.
Present this as a comparison table.

### Business Value
- Faster strategy evaluation
- Clear trade-off visibility
- Better investment decisions

---

## Demo 4: Turning Meetings into Action

### Scenario
Convert leadership meeting discussions into accountable actions.

### Copilot Prompt
Summarize key decisions and action items from yesterday’s leadership meeting.
Assign owners and suggested deadlines where possible.

### Business Value
- Improved execution
- Clear accountability
- Reduced follow-up effort

---

## Demo 5: Board-Ready Communication

### Scenario
Provide a concise AI adoption update to the board.

### Copilot Prompt
Draft a board-level update explaining our AI adoption progress.
Focus on business value, risks, and governance.
Limit to 5 bullet points and keep language non-technical.

### Business Value
- Clear executive communication
- Stronger stakeholder trust
- Faster reporting

# Microsoft Copilot for Microsoft 365  


### Audience
Business leaders and senior decision-makers

### Goal
Show how Copilot in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint helps leaders move faster from information to decisions.

---

## Demo 1: Word – Executive Strategy Brief

### Scenario
A leader needs a clear strategy summary from multiple internal documents.

### Copilot Prompt (Word)
Create a 1-page executive summary from this document.
Focus on strategic priorities, risks, and KPIs.
Use concise, leadership-level language.

### Business Value
- Faster strategy alignment  
- Clear communication  
- Less manual summarization  

---

## Demo 2: Word – Policy or Proposal Drafting

### Scenario
Draft a leadership-ready policy or proposal.

### Copilot Prompt (Word)
Draft a policy proposal based on this document.
Include objectives, key changes, business impact, and next steps.
Keep the tone formal and executive-ready.

### Business Value
- Accelerates governance and approvals  
- Consistent documentation  
- Reduces drafting effort  

---

## Demo 3: Excel – Performance Insights and Trends

### Scenario
Analyze business performance without deep Excel expertise.

### Copilot Prompt (Excel)
Analyze this data and identify:
- Top 3 performance trends
- Key risks or anomalies
- Suggested actions for leadership

Summarize insights in bullet points.

### Business Value
- Faster insight generation  
- Democratized data analysis  
- Better decision support  

---

## Demo 4: Excel – Forecasting and Scenario Modeling

### Scenario
Explore “what-if” scenarios for revenue or cost planning.

### Copilot Prompt (Excel)
Create a forecast for next quarter based on historical data.
Show best-case, expected, and worst-case scenarios.
Explain assumptions in simple language.

### Business Value
- Improved planning  
- Risk-aware decisions  
- Reduced reliance on analysts  

---

## Demo 5: PowerPoint – Board-Ready Presentation

### Scenario
Turn raw documents into a leadership presentation.

### Copilot Prompt (PowerPoint)
Create a 6-slide executive presentation from this document.
Include:
- Business context
- Key insights
- Financial impact
- Risks and mitigations
- Recommended actions

Use a clean, professional design.

### Business Value
- Faster board preparation  
- Consistent storytelling  
- Clear executive messaging  

---

## Key Leadership Takeaway
Copilot in Microsoft 365 helps leaders:
- Move faster from data to decisions  
- Scale expertise across the organization  
- Focus on strategy instead of manual work  


## Key Takeaway
Copilot Chat is a decision amplification tool that helps leaders move faster with confidence.
