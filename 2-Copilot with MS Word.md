# Microsoft Copilot with M365 Word 

Audience: Business Leaders  
Tool: Microsoft Copilot in Microsoft Word

---

## Use Case  
### Board-Ready Strategy Document Creation Using Copilot in Microsoft Word

### Why This Matters for Business Leaders
Senior leaders frequently need to:
- Convert fragmented thinking, meetings, and data into a clear, board-ready document
- Save time on structuring, wording, and narrative flow
- Ensure messaging is strategic, concise, and credible

Copilot in Microsoft Word helps leaders:
- Structure executive narratives
- Improve clarity and tone
- Stress-test logic and assumptions
- Accelerate drafting and revisions

## 10 High-Impact Copilot Prompts for Microsoft Word

### 1. Executive Structuring
**Prompt**  
Structure this document into a clear, board-ready strategy paper with an executive summary, strategic rationale, risks, options, and recommendation.

---

### 2. Executive Summary Creation
**Prompt**  
Write a one-page executive summary for board members who will only read the first page.

---

### 3. Strategic Assumption Identification
**Prompt**  
Identify the implicit strategic assumptions in this document and list them explicitly.

---

### 4. Risk Framing for the Board
**Prompt**  
Rewrite the risk section using board-level language, focusing on financial, operational, and reputational risk.

---

### 5. Option Comparison
**Prompt**  
Create a comparison of three strategic options: conservative entry, phased entry, and aggressive expansion, including pros, cons, and risk profile.

---

### 6. Recommendation Sharpening
**Prompt**  
Based on the document, draft a clear executive recommendation with rationale and conditions for success.

---

### 7. Language Simplification
**Prompt**  
Rewrite this document to remove jargon and make it clear to non-technical board members.

---

### 8. Challenge & Stress Test
**Prompt**  
Act as a skeptical board member and highlight weaknesses, unanswered questions, or logic gaps in this document.

---

### 9. Alignment Check
**Prompt**  
Check whether this strategy aligns with our stated growth rate, margin expectations, and execution capability. Highlight misalignments.

---

### 10. Final Polishing
**Prompt**  
Edit this document for executive presence, confidence, and conciseness while keeping it under five pages.

---

## Key Message for Leaders
Copilot in Word is not about writing faster.  
It is about thinking better, structuring decisions, and communicating with authority.
